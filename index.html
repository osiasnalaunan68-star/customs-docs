📋 Legal Basis & Compliance

  • CMTA (Customs Modernization and Tariff Act) – Primary customs law of the Philippines.
  • AHTN 2022 – Official tariff schedule adopted by the Bureau of Customs.
  • GIR 1 & 6 – General Rules for the Interpretation of the Harmonized System used for legal classification.
  • Documentary Sufficiency – For regulated goods (e.g., pure‑bred breeding animals), the system reminds users of required permits (SPSIC, pedigree, etc.) to avoid misdeclaration penalties.
  • Section 1400 – Misclassification can lead to 100‑300% surcharge; this tool helps you avoid that by providing clear classification guidance.

📖 Quick Start Guide

  1. Visit customs-v2-nu.vercel.app
  2. Register (or login if you already have an account) – email and password (min 6 characters).
  3. Explore the HS Lookup, Calculator, and AI Classifier tabs.
  4. Inject a tariff code from Lookup or AI into the Calculator to see the duty breakdown.
  5. Adjust values (CIF, duty rate) and use the Settings tab to apply EO overrides.
  6. Fetch Live to get the current USD/PHP exchange rate.
  7. Always cross‑check with official sources before making a final declaration.
